CHARDHAM YATRA

YAMUNOTRI - GANGOTRI -- KEDARNATH - BADRINATH

Day 01

Delhi

Arrival at Delhi , transfers to Haridwar , arrive and check in hotel . Rest of the day at leisure . Overnight at Haridwar . ( D )

Day 02

Barkot

After breakfast transfers to Barkot via Dehradun and Mussorie . On arrival at Barkot , Check in hotel . Overnight at Barkot . ( B.L.D. )

Day 03

Hanuman Chatti

Early morning Drive to Hanuman Chatti ( 32 KMS ) . From where , we will take a local Jeep for Phool chatti to Janki Chatti . We will start our trek from Janki Chatti for Yamunotri ( 5 KMS ) . Either by walk or by horse or by Doli at own cost . Arrival at Yamunotri , after taking bath in Jamunabai Kund's warm water and having " Darshan " of pious " Yamunaji " returning to Hanumanchatti . Later drive back to Barkot . Overnight at Barkot . ( B.L.D. )

Day 04

Uttarkashi

After breakfast check out from the hotel and Depart for Uttarkashi , arrival and check in hotel , evening visit the famous temple of Kashi - Viswnath . Overnight at Uttarkashi . ( B.L.D. )

Day 05

Gangotri

Early morning drive to Gangotri . Upon arrival at Gangotri take a holy dip in the River sacred The Ganges , It's also called Bhagirathi at its origin . Visit the temple . Late afternoon drive to Uttarkashi , overnight at Uttarkashi . ( B.L.D. )

Day 06

Guptkashi

Early morning Depart for Guptkashi Via , Tehri Dam , upon Check in hotel , Overnight at arrival at at Guptkashi Guptakashi . ( B.L.D. )

Day 07

Helipad

After early breakfast leave by vehicle up to Helipad as per the reporting time , do boarding formalities & wait here for your time slot , on arrival at Kedarnath perform early Pooja & Darshan at Temple & come back to Helipad to take return flight to Guptkashi , Dinner & overnight stay at Guptkashi . ( B.L.D. )

Day 08

Kedarnath

The day is reserved to visit Kedarnath by helicopter if not operated on previous day . Overnight stay at Guptkashi . ( B.L.D. )

Day 09

Rudraprayag / Pipalkoti

Early morning , transfers to Rudraprayag / Pipalkoti , arrive and check in Hotel . Overnight stay at Rudraprayag / Pipalkoti . ( B.L.D. )

Day 10

Badrinath

Morning transfers to Badrinath via Joshimath.On arrival Morning trans Check in to the Hotel . Further visit Mana village , Vyas Gufa , Maatamoorti , Charanpaduka , Bhimkund and the " Mukh " of the Saraswati River . Later at evening visit Badrinath Temple for Aarti . Overnight at Badrinath . ( B.L.D. )

Day 11

Srinagar

Early morning drive back to Srinagar . Arrive and check in hotel , overnight at Srinagar . ( B.L.D. )

Day 12

Rishikesh

Morning transfer to Rishikesh . En route visit Rishikesh local Sight Seeing like Laxman Jhula , Ram Jhula , Gita Bhavan and Raghunath Temple . Check in hotel . Evening visit Har - Ki - Pouri for Worship of Holy Ganges . Enjoy Ganga Arati , overnight at Haridwar . ( B.L.D. )

Day 13

home town

Depart for home town from Haridwar OR Delhi according to Train / Flight schedule . ( B. )
