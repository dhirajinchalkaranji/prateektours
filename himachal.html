SAMPOORNA HIMACHAL WITH SHAKTIPITH

Day 01

Chandigarh

Arrive at Chandigarh , check in hotel , Overnight at Chandigarh . ( D ).

Day 02

shimla

After breakfast transfers to shimla , en route visit Chandigarh sightseeing Rock garden , Sukhana lake , Pinjore garden . Overnight at Shimla . ( B.L.D. )

Day 03

Kufri

After Breakfast Excursion to Kufri . Kufri is famous for its Himalayan National Park , Poney and Yak Ride , evening is free to Explore Simla Mall road , overnight at Shimla . ( B.L.D. )

Day 04

Manali

Morning transfers to Manali , en route visit , Kulu shawl factory Reach evening in Manali . Overnight at Manali . ( B.L.D. )

Day 05

sightseeing visit

After breakfast sightseeing visit , Hadimba temple , Manu temple , Vashisht Bath , evening enjoy shopping at Mall road , overnight at Manall . ( B.L.D. )

Day 06

client can take the local State Transport

Excursion to Rohatang is not included in the itinerary because NGT ( National Green Tribunal ) has restricted the number of vehicle to this place and , It entirely depends upon the permission on a particular day . The client can take the local State Transport Bus ( @ INR 650 to 1250 per person return ) and visit Rohtang . The guest has to get the tickets from the Bus Depot at Mall Road . The local cabs charge somewhere between INR 5000 to INR 18000 depending on the demand .

Day 07

Dharamshala

After Breakfast transfers to Dharamshala . En route visit Chamunda devi temple . Arrive and check in hotel , overnight at Dharamshala , ( B.L.D. )

Day 08

Mc Leodgunj

After Breakfast visit , His holiness Dalai lama residence at Mc Leodgunj , War memorial , Bhagsunath temple and Dal Lake and also visit HPCA Stadium . Evening free at Leisure . Overnight at Dharamshala , ( B.L.D. )

Day 09

Dalhousie

After Breakfast transfers to Dalhousie . En route visit Jwalaji and Kangada temples . Arrive and check in hotel , overnight Dalhousie . ( B.L.D. )

Day 10

Khajjiar

After breakfast , excursion to Khajjiar 24 km from Dalhousie surrounded by thick Deodar forest . Drive back to Dalhousie , evening visit Ajit singh smarak and free time for relaxation , overnight at Dalhousie , ( B.L.D. )

Day 11

Pathankot or Jammu Tawi

Depart by rail from Pathankot or Jammu Tawi / Fly only from Jammu airport on Next day flight . ( B . )
