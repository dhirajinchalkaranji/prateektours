NEPAL WITH MUKTINATH

Day 01

Kathmandu

CB Day 09 Welcome receive at Kathmandu International Airport and transfers to hotel Check into hotel with welcome drink . Rest of the day is free for individual activities . Depend as per the arrival schedule , Overnight at Kathmandu ( B. )

Day 02

Pokhara Drive

After breakfast , departure to Pokhara Drive about & hours , check into hotel with welcome drink After Lunch and short rest at hotel , proceeds to Phewa Lake Boating about 1 hour to visit BARAHI Temple . Overnight at Pokhara . ( B.LD. )

Day 03

Pokhara domestic airport

Early in the morning transfer to Pokhara domestic airport , fly to Jomsom . After breakfast at Jomsom drive to Muktinath by Jeep , Ritual activities and Pooja with holy bath . At late Afternoon drive back to Jomsom overnight stay at hotel . ( B.L.D. )

Day 04

Pokhara

After breakfast , fly to Pokhara , start Pokhara city sightseeing including Davi's fall , Gupteshwor Cave , International Mountaineering Museum , White River Gorge , Bindhyawashini Temple . Rest of the day is free for self - excursion . Overnight at Pokhara . ( B.L.D. )

Day 05

Chitwan National Park

After breakfast , departure to Chitwan National Park . Arrive and check into hotel with welcome drink . In the evening Tharu ( ethnic ) Culture Dance . Overnight at Chitwan . ( B.L.D. )

Day 06

Jungle activities

After breakfast , start Jungle activities including : Canoeing on the Rapt River to see Crocodile / Jungle walk / Visit Elephants Breeding Center After lunch Elephant Safari to See One - Horned Rhinoceros and many Wild Animals , in the Evening visit Raph River Bank for Sunset View : Overnight at Hotel ( B.L.D. )

Day 07

Kathmandu

After breakfast , departure to Kathmandu . On the way visit Manakamana Devi's temple by cable car riding , check into hotel with welcome drink . Overnight stay at Kathmandu ( B.LD )

Day 08

Kathmandu valley

After breakfast , start Kathmandu valley sightseeing including Shree Pashupatinath Temple , Buddhanilkantha ( Sleeping Vistinu Swayambhunath Stupa . Bhaktapur City , evening is free for shopping . Overnight at Kathmandu ( B.L.D. )

Day 09

departure schedule

After breakfast , departure transfers to airport for final destination as per departure schedule. (D.)
