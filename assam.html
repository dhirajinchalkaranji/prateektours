THREE SISTERS

ASSAM- MEGHALAY- ARUNACHAL

Day 01

Guwahati

Arrival at Guwahati airport, arrive and check in hotel TO Aeg overnight at Guwahati. (B.L.D.)

Day 02

Kaziranga

Morning proceeds to Kaziranga, arrive and check in hotel Day 02 overnight at Kaziranga. (B.L.D.)

Day 03

Elephant safari

The day is free to enjoy Elephant safari / Jeep safari in kau Forest. Rest time is leisure. Overnight at Kaziranga. (B.LD)

Day 04

Bomdila

Early morning proceed to Bomdila, en route visit Tipi Orchidarium, a glass house with over 1000 Orchids. Arriva in evening, overnight at Bomdila. (B.L.D.)

Day 05

Tawang

Early morning proceed to Tawang. En route we visit The Sels Pass, Sela Lake, JashwantGarh (War Memorial). Arrive in evening, overnight at Tawang. (B.L.D.)

Day 06

Tawang Monastery

Morning visit Tawang Monastery, Tawang war memorial, rest day is leisure, overnight at Tawang. (B.L.D.)

Day 07

Bumla Pass & Sangestser Lake

The day is free to explore Bumla Pass & Sangestser Lake, popularly known as Madhuri Lake (Both sightseeing visits are subject to weather conditions & Military permissions) overnight at Tawang. (B.L.D.)

Day 08

Bomdila

Morning proceed to Bomdila. En route stop at the Nuranang falls, Dirang valley, arrive and check in at the hotel. Overnight at Bomdila. (B.L.D.)

Day 09

Tezpur

Morning proceed to Tezpur, arrive and check in at the hotel. Overnight at Tezpur. (B.L.D.)

Day 10

Shillong

Morning proceeds to Shillong, the Scotland of East. En route visit Barapani (Umiam lake). Arrive and check in at hotel. Overnight at Shillong. (B.L.D.)

Day 11

Cherrapunjee

Today we will explore Cherrapunjee, Shillong Peak, the Mawsmai Caves, Nohkalikai falls, Evening free for shopping. overnight at Shillong. (B.L.D.)

Day 12

Mawlynnong village

Today we explore Mawlynnong village (Awarded as 'cleanest village in India') later visit Balancing rock, Living Root Bridge which is made of the roots of a living tree. Back to Shillong overnight at Shillong. (B.L.D.)

Day 13

Guwahati

Morning proceed to Guwahati, en route visit Don Bosco Museum at Shillong, On arrival Guwahati visit Kamakhya Temple one of the 51 Shakti Peethas, rest day is leisure, overnight at Guwahati. (B.L.D.)

Day 14

Hometown

Depart for Hometown as per rail / air schedule. (B)
