LADAKH WITH KARGIL

Day 01

Leh

Arrival at Leh , check in hotel , the day is free to acclimatize , overnight at Leh . ( D . )

Day 02

The Hall of Fame

After breakfast visit the Hall of Fame ( A Museum developed by the Indian Army ) , Shanti Stupa and Leh palace , evening is free at leisure , overnight stay in Leh . , ( B.L.D . )

Day 03

Nubra Valley

After breakfast , drive to Nubra Valley ( Known as the Valley of Flowers and the warmest region in Ladakh ) via Khardungia pass . This road is considered to be the highest motorable road of the world . En route , admire the spectacular views of the never ending snow - capped mountain ranges around Khardungla Pass ( 18,380 feet ) after spending some time at Khardungla , proceed to Nubra . After Hotel check - in , enjoy camel Safari ( Optional ) in the evening . Overnight stay in Nubra . ( B.L.D . )

Day 04

Diskit & Hunder Villages

After breakfast , visit Diskit & Hunder Villages and monasteries take a stroll around the place to see the life style of people living at the northernmost region of India . Drive back to Leh via Khardungla pass . Evening is free to explore Leh local market . Overnight stay in Leh . ( B.L.D . )

Day 05

Pangong

After an early breakfast , proceed to Pangong via Changla Pass ( 37.595 food ( 17,586 feet ) and the third highest pass of the world . Pangong Lake is a salt water body of 120 km in length and 6-7 Km broad point.It is bisected by the international border between India & at the longest China ( 2/3 of the lake is in China's possession . 3 . After Lunch , travel back to Leh . Overnight stay in Leh . ( B.L.D . )

Day 06

Kargil

After breakfast check out and transfers to Kargil en route visit , Gurudwara Patthar Sahib ( A Gurudwara constructed & maintained by the Indian Army ) . Proceed further and experience the effects of Magnetic Hill ( Defiance of the law of gravity wherein a vehicle starts climbing up the hill when put in a neutral position ) and admire the confluence of holy Indus & Zanskar . Arrive in evening , overnight stay at Kargit . ( B.L.D . )

Day 07

Srinagar

Morning proceeds to Srinagar . En - route Visit War memorial , Zozila Pass , Sonmarg valley , arrive and check in hotel , overnight at Srinagar . ( B.L.D . )

Day 08

Hometown

Depart for hometown by air as per scheduled ( B. )
