AMRITSAR JAMMU KASHMIR

Day 01

Amritsar

Arrival at Amritsar , check in hotel , rest day is leisure , overnight at Amritsar . ( D )

Day 02

Golden temple

Morning Visit Golden temple and Jalianwala baug , evening visit Wagha boarder , overnight at Amritsar . ( B.L.D. )

Day 03

Katra

Morning transfers to Katra , arrive and check in hotel , overnight at Katra . ( B.L.D. )

Day 04

Mata Vaishnodevi Darshan

Early morning proceed for Mata Vaishnodevi Darshan . Reach Vaishnodevi by walk / horse / doli / Helicopter ( optional ) . After Darshan , return to Katra , overnight at Katra . ( B.L.D )

Day 05

Srinagar

Morning transfers to Srinagar , arrive and check in hotel , overnight at Srinagar . ( B.L.D. )

Day 06

Sri Shankaracharya Temple

After breakfast , visit Sri Shankaracharya Temple ( 1100 ft . above surface level of the main city on thehill . Climb 270 steps to reach the temple from where you would have a beautiful top angle view of Sri Nagar ) , Moughal Gardens I.e. Chashmeshahi - The Royal Spring , Nishat Baag - Garden of Bliss and Shalimar -Garden of Love . Evening , enjoy shikara ride in Dal Lake covering Nehru Park , Charchinar and Floating Market . Overnight at Srinagar hotel . ( B.L.D. )

Day 07

Hazratbal Durgah

After Breakfast check out from houseboat & check in hotel , later visit Hazratbal Durgah & proceed to Sonmarg ( Meadow of Gold ) , situated in the Sindh Valley . ( 8,950 ft . above the sea level ) to enjoy the Glaciers and Scenic Beauty on the Horseback ( optional ) . After lunch back to Srinagar , overnight stay at Srinagar hotel . ( B.L.D. )

Day 08

Gulmarg

Early morning proceed to Gulmarg ( 9,500 ft . above sea level ) From Gulmarg take Gandola Ropeway for Khilanmarg ( 10,050ft , above sea level ) . Experience the longest Cable Car ride up to the Affarwat snow point . En - route pass by the picturesque golf course . Free to play and enjoy in the snow . Back to hotelin the evening Overnight at Srinagar hotel . ( B.L.D. )

Day 09

Pahalgam

Morning transfers to Pahalgam , en route visit Avantipur ruins , Cricket bat factory and pass - by the Pampore Saffron fields . Enjoy shopping in the evening at Pahalgam .. Overnight at Pahalgam . ( B.L.D. )

Day 10

Hometown

Morning transfers to Srinagar / Jammu . Depart to your home town . ( B. )
